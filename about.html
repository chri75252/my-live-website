Our Story

The Blacksmith Market has evolved from a boutique distributor to one of the UK's most trusted wholesale partners for branded products.

Our Beginning

Founded in 2018, The Blacksmith Market began with a clear vision: to modernize wholesale distribution by connecting quality manufacturers with multiple retail channels through data-driven decision making.

Our founders recognized that many excellent branded products struggled to reach their full potential in the UK market due to distribution challenges. By creating a more flexible, responsive distribution model, we've been able to help both manufacturers and retailers thrive.

Today, we're proud to be a registered UK business with a growing presence across multiple product categories and retail channels.

Our Vision

To become the UK's most trusted and innovative wholesale distribution partner, creating seamless connections between quality products and profitable retail channels.

Our Mission

To drive growth for our partners through strategic product selection, efficient distribution, and exceptional service that creates value throughout the retail supply chain.
